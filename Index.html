Title:Love Beyond of the Wrong Time

Chapter 2: The Comeback

Author: Jhon Mark

Years passed, and Ryuo's life in his own dimension moved on. He pursued his dreams, built a successful career, and formed meaningful connections with others, but there was always a part of him that longed for the love he had left behind in that otherworldly dimension.

Meanwhile, in Nicole's realm, she dedicated herself to restoring the balance that had been disrupted by their love. With determination and resilience, she worked tirelessly to mend the rifts in her world. The once-vibrant colors began to return, and the floating islands once again found their equilibrium.

Yet, even as her dimension healed, Nicole couldn't forget Ryuo. She often gazed at the portal that had brought him into her life, hoping that one day, somehow, they might be reunited. The oracle's words echoed in her mind, reminding her of the power their love held—the power to change worlds.

One fateful day, as Ryuo was walking through a park in his own dimension, he noticed something peculiar. A shimmering, otherworldly portal had materialized before him. Without hesitation, he stepped through, hoping against hope that it would lead him back to Nicole.

To his astonishment, he found himself standing once again in Nicole's vibrant and surreal dimension. The very presence of his love seemed to strengthen the world around them. Colors became more vivid, and the floating islands drifted back together as if welcoming him home.

Nicole, who had been staring at the portal in wistful longing, couldn't believe her eyes when Ryuo stepped through. Tears of joy streamed down her cheeks as they embraced, their hearts beating in perfect harmony once more.

Their reunion sent ripples through the dimension, but this time, the world did not crumble. Instead, it flourished in the presence of their love, as if the universe itself had acknowledged the beauty and power of their connection.

Ryuo and Nicole, determined not to repeat the mistakes of the past, worked together to ensure the stability of her dimension. They harnessed the strength of their love to restore balance, using their experiences and knowledge to mend the fractures that had once threatened to tear the world apart.

As they walked hand in hand through Nicole's enchanting realm, they realized that their love, once seen as a disruption, had become a force of creation and renewal. It had transformed her dimension into a place of wonder and harmony, where love was not just a force to be reckoned with but the very essence of existence itself.

In their love, Ryuo and Nicole had discovered the ultimate truth—that love, when genuine and boundless, had the power to heal not only hearts but also worlds. Together, they had rewritten the rules of time and dimensions, proving that love could transcend all boundaries, bringing light, life, and love to the most unexpected of places.
